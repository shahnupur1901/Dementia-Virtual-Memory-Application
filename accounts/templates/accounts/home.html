Welcome to Solace

We aim to assist patients with dementia to reminiscence with an interactive photo gallery and contact their loved ones during emergencies

{% block nav-register %} Register here {% endblock %}

Our purpose is to provide our patients a connection with their identity and individuality.

We provide the following features :

  • Memory Gallery
  • Emergency Contact Facility
  • Information about themselves

Together, with the aid of love and technology, let's reduce the anxiety and pressure of these patientsone step at a time.

Our Features

Memory Gallery

This gallery comprises of memories (pictures, corresponding information and descriptions) segregated category-wise. We hope this helps in the memory retention of the patient.

Emergency Contact Facility

This module enables the user to send messages to all his/her’s emergency contacts and also specify his/her’s location.

About Me

This module is intended for reminding the patients of their individuality and identity. We wish that they’ll remember their loved ones and their old selves.
